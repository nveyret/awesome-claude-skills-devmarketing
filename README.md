## 🎯 ICP, Positioning & Messaging

- **[devtool-icp-segmentation](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Builds a structured ICP segmentation document for devtool companies using the Snowflake one-team GTM framework. Covers TAM/SAM, ABM tiers (1:1 / 1:few / 1:many), spearfishing accounts, and target account prioritization.

- **[devtool-persona-matrix](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Builds detailed buying persona cards and a "Magic Matrix" messaging framework for devtool companies. Maps motivations, objections, and messaging per persona — ideal for sales and marketing alignment.

- **[mkt1-homepage-positioning-review](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Checks a B2B startup's homepage against the MKT1 positioning framework. Evaluates clarity on who the product is for, what it does, and why it's better than alternatives.

- **[mkt1-marketing-advantages](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Helps B2B startup marketers identify and evaluate their marketing advantages using the MKT1 framework. Includes guided discovery for teams without existing advantage documentation.

## 📣 Demand Generation & ABM

- **[devtool-abm-play-designer](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Designs complete, time-bound ABM "plays" for devtool companies: competitive takeouts, event surround strategies, direct mail + SDR follow-up, reactivation campaigns, and signal-triggered outbound. Works with funding, job posting, intent, and competitor signals.

- **[devtool-content-ecosystem](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Builds a content ecosystem map and prioritized content plan for devtool companies. Audits existing content, identifies gaps by persona and funnel stage, and aligns content with ABM and SDR campaigns.

- **[mkt1-channel-strategy](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Identifies which of the 6 growth engines (marketing channels) best fit a B2B startup based on their PMM research, GTM motion, and marketing advantages. Uses the MKT1 channel-startup fit framework.

- **[mkt1-gaccs](https://github.com/nveyret/awesome-claude-skills-devmarketing)** — Builds a GACCS brief (Goals, Audience, Creative, Channels, Stakeholders) for any marketing initiative. Interactive guided session that produces a complete campaign brief.
